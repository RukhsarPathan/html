About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Rukhsar, and I am an aspiring front-end developer with a passion for technology and a strong foundation in web development.

For the past seven years, I have worked in the financial services sector, where I honed my analytical skills and developed a keen understanding of client needs. While I enjoyed my time in finance, I often felt that something was missing. It was during this period that I began to reflect on my childhood interests—my fascination with technology, starting from the days of Pascal and Windows 98.

From an early age, I was captivated by the world of computers. I loved creating no-code websites and even launched my own Shopify store, where I learned valuable lessons about user experience and design. These experiences ignited a deeper interest in web development and software engineering, leading me to pursue my Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering at Western Governors University (WGU).

At WGU, I have gained a solid foundation in programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My education has equipped me with the skills to create engaging and responsive web applications. I thrive on the challenge of transforming complex problems into intuitive, user-friendly solutions.

In addition to my technical skills, I enjoy learning about entrepreneurship and the journeys of influential tech leaders. Their stories inspire me as I work on my side projects, where I can experiment and apply my skills in real-world scenarios. I am eager to launch my career as a front-end developer, where I can combine my technical abilities with my background in finance to create meaningful digital experiences. My goal is to eventually expand my expertise to full-stack development, allowing me to contribute to every aspect of web projects.

When I’m not coding, you can find me exploring the latest tech trends, diving into personal projects that fuel my creativity, or reading about the innovations shaping our world. I believe in lifelong learning and am always looking for opportunities to grow and improve.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio! I invite you to explore my work and connect with me as I embark on this exciting journey in the world of web development.
